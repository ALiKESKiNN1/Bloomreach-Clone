Partnership Tiers

We work closely with our partners based on a system of mutual trust and support. The Bloomreach Partner Program offers three levels of partnership, reflecting the partner’s depth of technical expertise, training and certification, and business investment. Each level requires investment from partners and Bloomreach.

Silver

Entry-level benefits

  • Start your Bloomreach practice
  • Get basic training and certification
  • Gain solid product knowledge
  • Get support from Bloomreach experts
  • First Bloomreach project delivered

Gold

Entry-level benefits

  • Expand your Bloomreach practice
  • Advanced training and certification
  • Sales team enabled
  • Go-to-market planning
  • Solid implementation track record

Platinum

Entry-level benefits

  • Autonomous Bloomreach practice
  • Recognized for thought-leadership
  • Bloomreach sold as a preferred solution
  • Custom go-to-market planning
  • Track record of large-scale implementations
